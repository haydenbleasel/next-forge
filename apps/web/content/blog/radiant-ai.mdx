With Radiant AI, our mission is to make **AI more accessible** and useful, while maintaining transparency, ethics, and control over AI-powered outcomes.

## Why Radiant AI Matters

As AI continues to evolve, its role in our daily lives and work is becoming more profound. However, many AI solutions today are either too complex or too opaque for everyday users. That’s where **Radiant AI** comes in:
